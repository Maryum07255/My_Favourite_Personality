INTRODUCTION:

Muhammad Ali Jinnah, also known as Quaid-e-Azam (Great Leader), was a prominent lawyer, politician, and the founder of Pakistan. He was born on December 25, 1876, and passed away on September 11, 1948. Jinnah played a crucial role in the Pakistan Movement and served as the first Governor-General of Pakistan.
