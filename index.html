MY FAVOURITE PERSONALITY

INTRODUCTION:

My Favourite Personality is Muhammad Ali Jinnah . Quaid-e-Azam Muhammad Ali Jinnah is our National Hero . He is the founder of Pakistan.He is revered in Pakistan as the Quaid-e-Azam("Great Leader") and Baba-e-Qaum("Father of the Nation").His birthday is also observed as a national holiday in the world.

EARLY LIFE AND CAREER:

  • Born on December 25,1876, in ,Karachi, British India.

CHAMPION OF PAKISTAN
  1. JINNAH became the leader of the ALL-INDIA MUSLIM LEAGUE in 1913
  2. He championed the TWO NATION THEORY, which posited that Hindus and Muslims were distinct nations and deserved separate homelands.
  3. He led the MUSLIM LEAGUE'S CAMPAIGN for a separate Muslim state,culminating in the LAHORE RESOLUTION.

JINNAH'S DEATH:

Jinnah's Deathwas a significant loss for PAKISTAN,as he was revered as the "Quaid-e-Azam"(Great Laeder) and the"BABA-E-QUAM". His final days were spent grappling with the challenges of establishing a new Nation , which had taken a toll on his Health. Despite his failing health,he continued to work tirelessly for PAKISTAN, even during his periods of convalescence
